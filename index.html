About Me

I am a full stack web developer with a passion for creating functional, flexible, and friendly applications. Currently, I work for Austin Community College designing and implementing custom solutions for the faculty, staff, and students. Previously, I have taken a full stack web development bootcamp through the University of Texas and completed coursework in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin. I also graduated Summa Cum Laude from Texas A&M University with a Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical Engineering, and attended a doctoral program in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Washington.

While coding is my passion, I also enjoy playing tennis, thrifting, cooking, 3D printing, reading scifi/fantasy books, traveling, and exploring the Austin area. I enjoyed studying Japanese over the course of several semesters in college and would love to visit Japan in the future.
